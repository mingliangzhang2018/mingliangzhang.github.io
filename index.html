About me


I am a CS PhD student at the University of Notre Dame beginning from 2022 Spring. My supervisor is Professor Xiangliang Zhang.

Prior to Notre Dame, I got my B.E. degree in Computer Science at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) and my M.S degree in King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST).

My research interests include Natural Language Processing, Mathmatical Question Answering, Large Language Models, Multimodal Reasoning, etc.
